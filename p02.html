Culture of Elder

The culture of Elder range from city to country but there are some core philosophy that is the same through out the country.

Country Beliefs

Majority belices in the power of recording history to learn from. The reason for this belief is that so they country will not repeat simple mistakes and all pattern to the rise and fall of empires. They want to maintain their power on the world level for as long as possible.

What shaped this culture?

This belief was shaped by the two events of the decay of their world empire. The empire fell two times and on the third attempt made them realize that it may not be the best idea to maintain the power for long.

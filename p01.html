What kind of country is the starting country?

The starting country is an archipelago. An archipelago "is a chain, cluster, or collection of islands" (NOAA). If you would like to learn more about archipelagos and what constitute as an archipelago you can visit the National Ocean Service site and read all about them.

Country Map


Above is the first draft of the country map. The players will be able to explore the towns and cities in the country before traveling to surrounding areas. The landscape in the country will act as an obstacle for the party and they will need to figure out ways to traverse through the land.
